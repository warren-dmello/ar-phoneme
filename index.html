📋 Instructions
1
Open both markers using the links at the bottom. Display each one fullscreen on a screen, or print them. Hold your phone 20–30 cm away.
2
Allow camera access when your browser asks. The AR only works with the camera on.
3
Tap a phoneme pill at the bottom of the screen to hear its sound before you search for the marker.
4
Hold both markers in frame together — the word appears and is spoken aloud when the phonemes blend correctly.
5
On Hard mode, a third phoneme is shown. Listen to all three sounds before blending.

1

3
Tap either pill to hear the phoneme sound before searching for the marker.
4
Hold both markers in frame at the same time to blend the word.
5
Wrong combinations give a gentle nudge — no buzzer, just a redirect.
