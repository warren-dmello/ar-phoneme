How to Play
📋 Instructions
1
Get your two markers — print the Hiro and Kanji markers, or open them fullscreen on a second screen. Links are at the bottom.
2
Allow camera access when prompted. The AR only works with your camera on.
3
Point your camera at both markers at the same time to blend the phonemes into a word.
4
Tap the phoneme pills at the bottom of the screen to hear each sound played aloud before you look for the marker.
5
On Hard mode, a third phoneme is shown. You need to blend all three sounds to complete the word.

❓ How to Play

1
Point your camera at both printed markers at the same time.
2
The orange block is the consonant. The blue block is the vowel. Check the pills at the bottom to see which phonemes you need.
3
Tap either pill to hear the phoneme spoken aloud before you look for the marker.
4
When both markers are in frame together, the word appears and is spoken aloud. Tap 🔊 to hear it again.
5
Wrong combinations give a gentle redirect — no buzzer, just a nudge.
